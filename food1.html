About Us

FoodShare is a community-driven initiative that connects food donors with volunteers to efficiently distribute excess food to those in need. Our mission is to reduce food waste while addressing hunger in our communities.

Founded in 2022, we have successfully redistributed over 50,000 meals to homeless shelters, food banks, and directly to families experiencing food insecurity.

We believe that access to healthy food is a basic human right, and we are committed to creating a sustainable food system where no one goes hungry and no food is wasted.
